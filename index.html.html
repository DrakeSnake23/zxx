    Frequently Asked Questions

    Contact Us

    How does BlueTech quote projects?

    BlueTech offers an all-inclusive starter package for $399. This package includes a fully designed website, domain, SSL encryption, SEO optimization, AI integration, and custom design & creatives. This flat-rate package ensures transparency and value for our clients right from the start. For custom projects of larger scale, we provide custom quoting per project tailored for great solutions.

    What do I need to provide BlueTech to get a quote?

    Clients can choose our starter package, where we guide them through every detail needed to get started. Alternatively, for larger projects, clients can come to us with their vision, and well discuss how to bring it to life with a custom quote.

    What price model does BlueTech use?

    BlueTech offers two pricing models: our starter package, crafted for ultimate value at a flat rate, or a custom service model where pricing is based on time and materials needed to meet specific project requirements.

    How does BlueTech communicate?

    We prioritize transparency every step of the way. Our main communication methods are Google Meet, Zoom, and email, ensuring clear and consistent updates throughout the project.

    How does BlueTech handle feedback and changes?

    We prioritize customer satisfaction by handling feedback and changes free of charge, ensuring the final product meets your expectations without added costs.

    How many people are going to work on my project?

    For our starter package, two full-stack web developers and a project supervisor will work on your project. For custom projects, we first review the specifications, then allocate resources as needed to meet your unique requirements.
